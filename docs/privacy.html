Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Overview

SkipIt is designed with your privacy in mind. We believe in complete transparency about how our extension operates.

Data Collection

  • SkipIt does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data
  • No user behavior or viewing habits are tracked
  • No analytics or tracking systems are implemented

Storage Usage

The only data stored are your service preferences (Netflix, Disney+, Amazon Prime Video), which are:

  • Stored locally in your browser using Chrome's storage sync API
  • Only used to remember your streaming service toggle preferences
  • Never transmitted to external servers

Permissions

SkipIt requires minimal permissions to function:

  • "activeTab" - To detect and click skip buttons on streaming services
  • "storage" - To save your streaming service preferences locally
